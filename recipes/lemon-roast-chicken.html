Lemon Roast Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1-2 tbsp Maggi Magic Mix
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 tsp paprika powder
  • 2 lemons
  • 4 white onions
  • 3-4 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Extract the zest from the lemons before cutting them in half.
  2. Extract the juice form the lemon halves.
  3. Combine Magic Mix, paprika powder, lemon zest and lemon juice in a bowl.
  4. Poke holes in the skin of the chicken.
  5. Place chicken in a ziplock bag and pour the combined marinade on the meat. Leave in the refrigerator for at least an hour.
  6. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
  7. Arrange the marinated chicken on a baking pan.
  8. Bake the chicken for 60 minutes, turning the chicken once after 30 minutes.
  9. Take the chicken out of the oven and serve.
