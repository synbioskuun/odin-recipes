Spaghetti

Ingredients:

Spaghetti pasta:

  • 500g spaghetti noodles
  • Pinch of salt
    • 2-3 tbsp oil

Spaghetti sauce:

  • 250g ground pork or beef, seasoned with salt and pepper
  • 1 pack of sweet spaghetti sauce
  • 2 red onions, chopped
  • 3 pieces of garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatos, sliced into chunks
  • butter or oil
  • 1-2 tbsp brown sugar

Instructions:

Spaghetti pasta:

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil. Add salt and oil.
  2. Once water is boiling, add the spaghetti noodles.
  3. Once noodles are al dente, turn off heat and drain water.
  4. Pour cold water on cooked noodles to keep them al dente.
  5. Set noodles aside.

Spaghetti sauce:

  1. Saute onions and garlic in oil or butter.
  2. Add tomatos and saute until tomatos are soft.
  3. Add ground pork/beef and brown sugar.
  4. Cook until meat turns brown.
  5. Pour 1 pack of sweet spaghetti sauce.
  6. Simmer for 3-4 minutes in medium-low heat or until sauce begins to boil.
  7. Add cooked spaghetti noodles and enjoy.
